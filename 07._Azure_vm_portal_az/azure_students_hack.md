## Steps to Sign Up for Azure Student Account

1. **Create an account with a private/burner email**: Sign up using a personal or temporary email address in Azure.
2. **Follow the student sign-up link**: Go to [Azure for Students](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students/) and start the sign-up process.
3. **Fill out the sign-up form**: The form will look different when logged in with a private/burner email. You can manually enter your school email address, which will only be used for authentication.
4. **Submit the form**: Complete and submit the sign-up form.
5. **Check your school email**: Log in to your school email account to find a confirmation message.
6. **Confirm your email**: Follow the instructions in the email to confirm your account.
7. **Gain access**: Once confirmed, you will receive access to the Azure student package.
